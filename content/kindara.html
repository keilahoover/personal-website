Fertility Awareness App -- Added Endometriosis & Symptom Features

PROBLEMSOLVED

I had the privilege of working with, Kindara, a company with a mission to offer women the tools, knowledge, and support to understand how their fertility works, take ownership of their reproductive health, and meet their fertility goals. I reached out to the Kindara team to work together in adding awareness around a health condition that affects over 200 million women worldwide, Endometriosis.

I also felt it prudent to add symptom data tracking to keep up with their competitors and to over all enhance UX.

Customer Retention increased by adding Endometriosis Awareness Features to the Kindara app, existing and new user alike will be able to understand their bodies even better, and create a community to support each other.

Sales Adding Symptom tracking before the custom data will increase a chance of leading the user to upgrade to a subscription.

FEATURES ADDED

  • Symptoms Data Tracking
  • Symptoms Reminder
  • Endometriosis Community
  • Endometriosis Awareness Building Features
