Fertility Awareness App -- Added Endometriosis & Symptom Features

I had the privilege of working with, Kindara, a company with a mission to offer women the tools, knowledge, and support to understand how their fertility works, take ownership of their reproductive health, and meet their fertility goals.

I reached out to the Kindara team to work together in adding awareness around a health condition that affects over 200 million women worldwide, Endometriosis. I also felt it prudent to add symptom data tracking to keep up with their competitors and to over all enhance UX.

FEATURES ADDED

  • Symptoms Data Tracking
  • Symptoms Reminder
  • Endometriosis Community
  • Endometriosis Awareness Building Features
