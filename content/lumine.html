An app to find antiques, vintage, and collectible items.

An arcade platform game. As the developer and designer of the game, I made sure the UI flowed well enough that the game spoke for itself via the art, music, and experience. Tyler Davis, the Digital Artist, helped invision and create the digital assets.

On an unknown planet, Lumine the Alien has taken on the mission to collect as many crystals before his world is taken over by asteroids. Help him collect all the crystals!

HOW TO PLAY

CONTROLS

  • To Move: left & right arrow keys
  • To Jump: up arrow key
  • To Mute Music: Press M
  • To Resume/Play Music: Press P
  • To Restart Game: Cmd + R
