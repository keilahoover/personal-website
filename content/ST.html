RESUMÉ KEILA HOOVER KINDARA PROJECT

SOUNDS TRUE

Multimedia Publishing Company with the Largest Living Library of Transformational Teachings That Support and Accelerate Spiritual Awakening and Personal Transformation

PROBLEM SOLVED

In an ever changing world, Sounds True needed to enhance and update branding and online presence. I had the privilege in collaborating with the UX Design team to create a better user experience. I lead the redesign for the product pages, along with collaborating on the entirety of the website. After a number of scrum meetings and changes, the website redesign was launched in Autumn 2018.

View Website

PRODUCT PAGE RE-DESIGN

More User Appealing & Web Friendly Product Image

After working with the design team for months to clean up the company's image archive, all product pages now have consistent, clean imagery.

Modernized Branding and Design

Collaborated with the design team to create a new logo and modernize the the layout and color palette.

Wishlist Bug Fixed & Enhanced

Once the user clicked on “Add to Wishlist,” they were not notified or directed to the wishlist. Now the user is directed to their wishlist, and have an option to continue shopping by clicking the wishlist’s CTA.

Social Media Share -- OAuth

User can now share the product to Facebook and/or Twitter

Enhanced Review User Experience

The user can now see an average star review, vote whether they felt the review was helpful, and sort by date or average rating, and share review to Facebook and/or Twitter.

Search Optimization

A user can now more intuitively find a product that fits their needs. Through much data analysis, my team and I were able to lessen the confusion created by little to non-existent SEO and improve the user experience.
